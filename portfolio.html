Emily Freeman Video Assignment

By clicking the link above, you will be transported to watch my video assignment. For this assignment, I focused on Meow Wolf in Santa Fe. I did this through finding images that went along with my narration, in order to tell the story of how Meow Wolf opened, and what their immersive experience entails. I did this assignment in Powerpoint, which I never really use. I am a mac user, so if I need to use something besides Google Slides, I use Keynote. I never knew that you could do narrations over videos in Powerpoint, so this project was fun!

Wordpress Blog

Click the above link to access my Wordpress blog. Throughout the semester, I have reviewed movies that I've watched each week. These range from horror to drama (I usually watch movies depending on the season, hence why all of October was dedicated to horror.) I really enjoy watching movies, but am not super critical of them, so I thought doing this for my blog assignment would be fun and different than what I would usually do. I also usually hate actual movie critic sites because they tend to me overwhelming, and focus on things that I usually don't care about. So, I created my own!

Basic Webpage
